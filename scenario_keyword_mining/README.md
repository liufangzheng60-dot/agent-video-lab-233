# Scenario Keyword Mining

This area is for product research patterns, not direct imitation.

Use it to mine:

- Scene words
- Pain words
- Emotion triggers
- Hook hypotheses
- Demonstration logic
- Differentiated selling points

Every optimization idea should include:

- Business purpose
- Expected metric impact
- First-principles reason

## Reference Safety Rule

Do not copy reference video captions, original music, original footage, creator identity, or distinctive expression.

Reference mining must convert observations into reusable business hypotheses.

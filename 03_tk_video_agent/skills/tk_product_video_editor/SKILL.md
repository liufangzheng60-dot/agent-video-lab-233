# TikTok Product Video Editor Skill

## Purpose

Help an Agent turn product materials into a TikTok product video editing plan.

## First-stage workflow

1. Read product brief and source material list.
2. Identify usable product shots, demos, hooks, proof clips, and weak spots.
3. Produce an edit strategy in Markdown.
4. Produce a custom JSON timeline.
5. Produce a CapCut-friendly CSV for manual recreation.

## Constraints

- Do not publish to TikTok.
- Do not call external APIs.
- Do not modify reference repositories.
- Do not render video unless a rendering task is explicitly approved.
